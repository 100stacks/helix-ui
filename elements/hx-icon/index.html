Styling

NOTE Icons inherit the color and background-color of their parent.

Styling Icons is similar to styling text. You can change properties like, font-size, color, background-color, height, width, etc. 

<hx-icon type="times-circle"></hx-icon>
<hx-icon class="size-2x" type="times-circle"></hx-icon>
<hx-icon class="size-3x danger" type="times-circle"></hx-icon>
